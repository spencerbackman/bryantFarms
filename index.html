World Class Flavor.

At Bryant Farms our cattle are raised how they should be. They are pastured raised grass fed cows until the last 4-5 months when they are finished with a mix of grass & grain. Switching them to grain fattens the cattle up, making their meat juicy with an exquisite taste. Know what you're feeding your family, antibiotic-free, hormone-free, and gmo-free natural beef.
